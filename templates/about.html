About Us

Welcome to the IIIT College Mess Weekly Food Safety Checklist page!

Our updated system aims to enhance food safety standards in the mess facilities at our institute. We have introduced a new way to manage and review safety records, making the process more efficient and user-friendly.

Key features of the updated system include:

  1. Customizable Checklists: You can now add custom fields to the checklist based on your needs.
  2. Category-Based Fields: Fields are categorized into Personal Hygiene, Cleaning & Sanitization, Food Storage, Cooking & Serving, Pest Control, Equipment Maintenance, and Waste Management.
  3. Easy Record Management: Add, edit, and view records with ease through our intuitive interface.

We are committed to maintaining high food safety standards and improving the process continually. If you have any feedback or suggestions, please reach out to us through the Contact page.

Thank you for supporting our mission to ensure a safe and healthy dining environment!
